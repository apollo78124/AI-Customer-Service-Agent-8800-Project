
## **Chemical and Environmental Technology (Process Engineering Option), Diploma, Full-time (537BDIPLT) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Full-time Diploma in Process Engineering**.  
- **Delivery:** **In-person (Burnaby Campus)**.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Trains students in **chemical & biochemical process operations**.  
  - Covers **chemical engineering principles, process simulation, and materials technology**.  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Process Engineering & Chemical Technology**  
- **Industrial Process Optimization & Control**  
- **Water Treatment & Mineral Processing**  
- **Process Simulation & Safety Standards**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intake:** **September**.  
- **Duration:** **2 years (4 levels, including practicum & applied research project)**.  
- **Schedule:** **Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM**.  
- **Work Placement:** **Mandatory industry practicum (Co-op Work Permit required for international students).**  

### **Core Courses**
#### **Year 1 (Common Core with Analytical Science)**
- **CENV 1101 - Safety & Technology Workshop**  
- **CENV 1119 - Environmental Science**  
- **CHEM 1121 - General Chemistry**  
- **PHYS 1181 - Physics for Chemical & Environmental Tech**  
- **MATH 1412 - Technical Mathematics**  

#### **Year 2 (Process Engineering Specialization)**
- **CENV 3300 - Bioprocess Engineering**  
- **CENV 3341 - Chemical Engineering Technology 1**  
- **CENV 3346 - Renewable Resources & Paper Production**  
- **CENV 4403 - Process Simulation**  
- **CENV 4414 - Mineral Processing**  
- **CENV 4419 - Instrumental Analysis for Process Engineering**  
- **CENV 4401 - Industry Practicum**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **English 12 (67%) & Math 12 (73%) or equivalent**.  
- **Chemistry 11 (73%)**.  
- **International Students:** **Study permit required + Co-op Work Permit for practicum**.  

### **Career Paths**
- **Chemical Process Engineer (Technologist Level)**  
- **Production & Process Optimization**  
- **Materials & Waste Management**  
- **Water Treatment & Environmental Compliance**
