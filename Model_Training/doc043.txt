
## **Cybersecurity Operations, Microcredential, Part-time (0832CM) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Cybersecurity Operations Microcredential**  
- **Delivery:** **Fully Online (Part-time, Self-Paced)**  
- **Objective:**  
  - Provides foundational training in **Security Operations Center (SOC) tasks**  
  - Prepares students for roles such as **Security Analyst or Incident Responder**  
  - Develops skills in **network attack detection, cybersecurity analysis, and incident response planning**  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Network Attack Surfaces & Risk Analysis**  
- **Threat Detection & Cybersecurity Defense**  
- **Incident Response & Forensic Data Analysis**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**  
- **Completion timeline:** **Self-paced, part-time study**  
- **Total Credits:** **6.0**  

### **Core Courses**
- **XINC 2110 - Fundamentals of System and Network Attack Surfaces (2.0 credits)**  
- **XINC 2120 - Fundamentals of Cybersecurity Threats and Attacks (1.0 credit)**  
- **XINC 2130 - Fundamentals of Cybersecurity Network Defence (1.0 credit)**  
- **XINC 2140 - Fundamentals of Cybersecurity Data Analysis (2.0 credits)**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Recommended:** Prior IT education, diploma, degree, or cybersecurity certification  
- **International Students:** **Eligible if studying from outside Canada (not for study permit holders)**  

### **Career Paths**
- **Security Analyst (SOC Analyst)**  
- **Cyber Incident Responder**  
- **Network Security Engineer**  
- **Threat Intelligence Analyst**
