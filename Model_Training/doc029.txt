## **Building Controls & Energy Management, Advanced Certificate, Part-time (5095ADCERT) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Advanced Certificate in Energy & Building Automation**.
- **Delivery:** **Online (Part-time)**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Teach professionals how to **optimize energy efficiency through building control systems**.
  - Develop expertise in **HVAC, automation, commissioning, and building analytics**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Building Automation & Energy Management Systems**  
- **HVAC Control Optimization**  
- **Building Commissioning & Performance Analytics**  
- **Energy Codes, Regulations & Compliance**  
- **IT Integration & Smart Building Technologies**  

### **Core Courses**
- **CESA 5100 - Energy Basics**
- **CESA 5320 - Energy Systems & Controls**
- **CESA 5420 - Building System Integration & Analytics**
- **CESA 5500 - Codes and Standards**
- **CESA 5620 - Commissioning & Optimization**
- **CESA 5700 - Decision Making in Energy Management**
- **CESA 5800 - Change Management in Smart Buildings**
- **CESA 5820 - Controls & Operations Management**
- **CESA 5920 - Final Controls Project**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Pre-entry Assessment Form required**.  
- **Math 11 & English 12 (67%) or equivalent**.  
- **Relevant diploma, degree, or Red Seal trade certification**.  
- **Industry experience in building operations or facilities management preferred**.  
- **International Students:** **Eligible for online completion (not for study permit holders).**
