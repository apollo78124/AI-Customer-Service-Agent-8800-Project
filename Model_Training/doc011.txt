
## **Applied Data Analytics, Certificate, Part-time (5512CERT) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Technical Data Analytics Training**.
- **Delivery:** **Blended (Online & On-Campus)**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Develop expertise in **data modeling, SQL, Python, Excel, Business Intelligence (BI), and visualization**.
  - Prepare students for **data-driven decision-making roles**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Database Management & SQL**
- **Python for Data Analytics**
- **Business Intelligence (BI) Tools (Power BI, Tableau)**
- **Statistics & Data Quality Improvement**
- **IT Security & Legal Considerations**

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**.
- **Completion timeline:** **2-5 years**.
- **Schedule:** **Weekday evenings & weekends**.

### **Core Courses**
#### **Tier 1: Foundations**
- **COMP 1630 - Relational Database Design & SQL**
- **COMP 2364 - Microsoft Excel for Data Analysis**
- **MATH 1060 - Statistics for Data Analytics**

#### **Tier 2: Core Data Skills**
- **COMP 2853 - Python for Data Analysis**
- **COMP 2831 - Business Analysis & Systems Design**
- **COMP 3839 - Data Quality Improvement**
- **COMP 2156 - Microsoft Power BI Fundamentals**

#### **Tier 3: Advanced Topics**
- **COMP 3704 - Applied IT Security**
- **COMP 2256 - Visual Analytics with Tableau**
- **MATH 3060 - Advanced Statistical Techniques**

#### **Tier 4: Specialization & Capstone**
- **COMP 4254 - Advanced Topics in Data Analytics**
- **BLAW 3205 - Internet & IT Law**
- **Final Capstone Project**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Prerequisites:**  
  - **Basic computing & Windows skills (COMP 1002 recommended)**.  
  - **Mathematics proficiency (pre-calculus or MATH 0060 refresher suggested)**.  
- **International Students:**  
  - **Study permit required**.
