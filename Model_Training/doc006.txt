
## **Applied Computer Science (Games Development Option), Bachelor of Science, Full-time (867ABSC) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Specialized Bachelor's Degree in Game Development**.  
- **Delivery:** **Full-time (In-person, Burnaby Campus)**.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Train students in **game design, artificial intelligence, physics, and 3D graphics programming**.  
  - Develop **strong theoretical knowledge and practical development skills** using tools like **Unity3D, OpenGL, and C++**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Game Engine Development**  
- **3D Graphics & Physics-Based Simulation**  
- **Artificial Intelligence for Games**  
- **Game Networking & Multiplayer Systems**  
- **Audio-Visual Effects & User Interface Design**

### **Program Structure**
- **Intake:** **September**.  
- **Duration:** **2 years (Four 15-week terms)**.  
- **Class schedule:** **Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM**.  
- **Co-op Option:** **Competitive, optional placements available**.  

### **Core Courses**
#### **Level 5 (15 weeks)**
- **COMP 7035 - Operating Systems (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 7051 - Introduction to Game Development (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 7082 - Software Engineering (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 7903 - Game Design Fundamentals (3.0 credits)**  
- **MATH 7808 - Calculus for Computing (4.0 credits)**  

#### **Level 6 (15 weeks)**
- **COMP 7905 - Art in Game Development (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 8042 - Advanced Algorithms & Data Structures (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 8051 - Advanced Game Architecture (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 8082 - Project Management (3.0 credits)**  
- **MATH 7908 - Linear Algebra for Computing (4.0 credits)**  

#### **Level 7 (15 weeks)**
- **COMP 7003 - Introduction to Information & Network Security (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 8085 - Artificial Intelligence (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 8552 - Advanced Game Programming Techniques (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 8800 - Major Project 1 (3.0 credits)**  
- **LIBS 7001 - Critical Reading & Writing (3.0 credits)**  
- **LIBS 7002 - Applied Ethics (3.0 credits)**  

#### **Level 8 (15 weeks)**
- **COMP 7012 - Interaction Design (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 8900 - Major Project 2 (3.0 credits)**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Prerequisites:**  
  - **60.0 credits of post-secondary education in computing** (BCIT CST Diploma or equivalent).  
  - **Mathematics & Statistics coursework required**.  
  - **Experience in C++ recommended**.  
- **International Students:**  
  - **Study permit required**.  
  - **Co-op work permit required for work experience**.

### **Career Paths**
- **Game Developer** (Indie or AAA Studios)  
- **Game AI Programmer**  
- **Graphics & Physics Programmer**  
- **AR/VR Developer**  
- **Game Network Engineer**
