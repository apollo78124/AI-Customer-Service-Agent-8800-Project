
## **Assayer Training, Statement of Completion, Part-time (5360NOBCIT) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Mineral Assaying Training Program**.
- **Delivery:** **Online with Lab Practicums**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Train students in **mineral identification, fire assaying, and wet analysis**.
  - Prepare graduates for **BC Assayer Certification**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Fire Assaying & Mineral Identification**
- **Wet Chemical Analysis & Instrumentation**
- **Sampling Theory & Statistical Methods**
- **Quality Control & Assay Reporting**

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **Ongoing flexible intake**.
- **Completion timeline:** **1.5 - 2 years**.
- **Schedule:** **Online learning with lab practicums**.

### **Core Courses**
- **CHSC 5631 - Introduction to Assayer Training (3.0 credits)**
- **CHSC 5632 - Mineral Identification & Fire Assaying (3.0 credits)**
- **CHSC 5633 - Wet Chemical Assay & Qualitative Analysis (3.0 credits)**
- **CHSC 5634 - Analytical Instrumentation (3.0 credits)**
- **CHSC 5635 - Laboratory Practicum (3.0 credits)**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Prerequisites:**  
  - **Diploma in Chemical or Mining Technology (or equivalent work experience)**.  
  - **Basic Chemistry & Mathematics knowledge required**.  
- **International Students:**  
  - **Eligible if completing from outside Canada or with a valid work permit (not eligible for study permit holders)**.
