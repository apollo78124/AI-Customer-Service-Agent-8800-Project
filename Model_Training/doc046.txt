
## **Essentials of Data Networks, Microcredential, Part-time (0811CM) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Microcredential in Networking Fundamentals**  
- **Delivery:** **Fully Online (Part-time, Self-Paced)**  
- **Objective:**  
  - Introduces **basic networking concepts**  
  - Prepares students for **entry-level networking roles**  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Network Fundamentals & Terminology**  
- **IP Addressing & Network Protocols**  
- **Network Connectivity & Security Basics**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**  
- **Completion timeline:** **Self-paced, part-time**  
- **Total Credits:** **6.0**  

### **Core Courses**
- **XINC 1101 - Network Terminology and Protocols (2.0 credits)**  
- **XINC 1102 - Network Addressing and Services (2.0 credits)**  
- **XINC 1103 - Network Connectivity (2.0 credits)**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Minimum Requirement:** Grade 10 completion  
- **Recommended:** Basic computer literacy & web browsing skills  
- **International Students:** **Eligible if studying from outside Canada (not for study permit holders)**  

### **Career Paths**
- **Network Administrator (Entry-Level)**  
- **Data Center Technician**  
- **IT Support Specialist**
