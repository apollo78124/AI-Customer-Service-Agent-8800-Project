
## **Applied Web Development, Associate Certificate, Part-time (6957ACERT) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Front-End & Back-End Web Development Specialization**.
- **Delivery:** **Blended (Online & On-Campus)**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Train students to develop **fully functional web applications**.
  - Cover **HTML, CSS, JavaScript, UX/UI, and server-side programming**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **HTML, CSS, & JavaScript Fundamentals**
- **Full-Stack Development (Node.js, React, PHP)**
- **Database-Driven Web Applications**
- **UX/UI Design & Business Analysis**
- **Front-End & Back-End Web Technologies**

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**.
- **Completion timeline:** **1-3 years**.
- **Schedule:** **Evening & weekend courses**.

### **Core Courses**
- **COMP 1630 - Relational Database & SQL (5.0 credits)**
- **COMP 1850 - Introduction to Web Development (3.0 credits)**
- **COMP 2132 - JavaScript for Web Development (3.0 credits)**
- **COMP 2831 - Business Analysis & Systems Design (4.0 credits)**

### **Elective Courses (Choose 9.0 Credits)**
- **COMP 1011 - UX/UI Development**
- **COMP 2052 - Android & Mobile App Development**
- **COMP 2909 - Front-End with Angular & Vue.js**
- **COMP 3012 - Back-End with Node.js**
- **COMP 3013 - Front-End with React.js**
- **COMP 3015 - PHP Web Application Development**
- **COMP 4669 - Advanced PHP Web Applications**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Prerequisites:**  
  - **Basic computing knowledge (COMP 1002 recommended)**.  
  - **Mathematics & English proficiency (Grade 12 or equivalent)**.  
- **International Students:**  
  - **Not available for study permit holders**.
