
## **Computer Information Technology, Diploma, Full-time (5540DIPMA) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Full-time Diploma in IT Systems & Software Development**.
- **Delivery:** **In-person (Downtown Campus, BCIT TEC Hub)**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Provides comprehensive training in **IT infrastructure, networking, web development, and software programming**.
  - Equips students with **hands-on experience in managing IT systems and application development**.
  - Offers **co-op and industry-sponsored projects**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **IT Infrastructure & Networking**
- **Database Management & SQL**
- **Web & Mobile Application Development**
- **Agile Software Development & UX/UI**
- **Windows & Linux System Administration**
- **Cloud Computing & DevOps**

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **September & January**.  
- **Duration:** **2 years (4 levels, 120 credits total)**.  
- **Schedule:** **Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM**.  
- **Co-op & Practicum:** **Industry placements available (competitive entry).**  

### **Core Courses**
#### **Year 1 (Foundation)**
- **ACIT 1420 - Introduction to Systems Administration**
- **ACIT 1515 - Scripting for IT (Python, PowerShell)**
- **ACIT 1620 - Fundamental Web Technologies**
- **ACIT 1630 - Database Systems & SQL**
- **COMM 1116 - Business Communications**
- **MATH 1310 - Technical Math for IT**
- **ORGB 1100 - Organizational Behavior**

#### **Year 2 (Specialization & Industry Application)**
- **ACIT 2420 - Linux System Administration**
- **ACIT 2515 - Object-Oriented Programming (Java)**
- **ACIT 2520 - Web Application Development**
- **ACIT 2620 - Enterprise Networking**
- **ACIT 2811 - UX/UI Development**
- **ACIT 2900 - IT Projects Practicum**
- **ACIT 4630 - Cybersecurity & Information Assurance**
- **ACIT 4640 - Network & System Provisioning**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **English 12 (67%) & Math 11 (60%) or equivalent**.  
- **International Students:** **Eligible with a study permit**.  

### **Career Paths**
- **Network Administrator**
- **Systems Analyst**
- **Cloud Engineer**
- **IT Security Specialist**
- **Web & Mobile Developer**
- **Opportunities for Industry Certifications (CCNA, AWS, Azure, Linux, RHCSA)**

- **Type:** **Part-time Certificate in Computing & Software Development**.  
- **Delivery:** **Blended (Evening & Weekend Classes)**.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Provides foundational skills in **programming, web development, and IT infrastructure**.  
  - Prepares students for **CST Diploma (Direct Entry Level 3 option available)**.  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Programming in Python, Java, and C**
- **Database & SQL**
- **Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)**
- **Systems Analysis & Business Communication**
- **Computer Architecture & Networking Basics**

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**.  
- **Completion timeline:** **2-3 years (Flexible Learning).**  

### **Core Courses**
- **COMP 1516 - Programming Fundamentals (Python)**
- **COMP 1630 - Database Design & SQL**
- **COMP 2121 - Discrete Mathematics**
- **COMP 2511 - Procedural Programming (C)**
- **COMP 2831 - Business Analysis & Systems Design**
- **COMP 2833 - Agile Scrum Methods**
- **COMP 2825 - Computer Architecture**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
