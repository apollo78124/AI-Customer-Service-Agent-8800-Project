
## **Entrance Requirements - BCIT**
### **General Admission Process**
- **Program-Specific Requirements:** Each program has unique admission criteria  
- **Assessing Previous Studies & Experience:**  
  - **Credential Evaluation:** Determines equivalency of non-BC educational credentials  
  - **Midterm Grades:** Some programs allow applications with midterm grades  

### **Key Admission Areas**
- **English Proficiency Standards:**  
  - **Minimum requirements based on program category**  
  - **Equivalencies accepted from international high school or post-secondary education**  
- **Assessment Testing:** Required if upgrading academic credentials  
- **Transfer Credit:** Available for recognized prior learning  

### **Study Permit Requirements**
- **International students must submit a valid study permit before program start**  
- **Check program eligibility for study permit acceptance**
