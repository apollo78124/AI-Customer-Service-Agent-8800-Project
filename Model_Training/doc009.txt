
## **Applied Computer Science (Wireless and Mobile Applications Development Option), Bachelor of Science, Part-time (867FBSC) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Wireless & Mobile Application Development Specialization**.
- **Delivery:** **Blended (On-campus & Online)**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Train students to develop **mobile applications for smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, and wireless devices**.
  - Covers **WiFi, Bluetooth, NFC, mobile computing constraints (battery, memory, CPU usage)**.
  - Hands-on development using **Android, iOS, and cross-platform frameworks**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Mobile UI/UX Design & Interaction**
- **Wireless Communication & Security**
- **Optimizing Performance & Battery Life**
- **IoT & Wearable Technology Integration**
- **Network Protocols & Cloud Integration**

### **Program Structure**
- **Flexible Learning:** Course-by-course registration.
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**.
- **Completion timeline:** **Up to 7 years**.
- **Schedule:** **Evenings & weekends** (Downtown/Burnaby campus & online).
- **Graduate Studies Pathway:** Prepares students for **BCITâ€™s MSc in Applied Computing**.
