

## **Business Information Technology Management (Analytics Data Management Option), Diploma, Full-time (623CDIPMA) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Full-time Diploma in Business IT & Data Analytics**.
- **Delivery:** **In-person (Burnaby Campus)**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Provide expertise in **data governance, quality management, and data integration**.
  - Train students to **transform raw data into business insights** for analytics and decision-making.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Data Governance & Security**  
- **Data Quality & Integration Techniques**  
- **Business Intelligence & Predictive Analytics**  
- **Cloud Computing for Business**  
- **Management Information Systems (MIS)**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intake:** **September**.  
- **Duration:** **2 years (4 levels, 115.5 total credits)**.  
- **Schedule:** **Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM**.  

### **Core Courses**
#### **Level 3 (Data Analytics Foundations)**
- **BABI 3000 - Data Quality Approaches for Business**
- **BABI 3005 - Data Governance for Business Analytics**
- **BSYS 3000 - Cloud Business Development 1**
- **BSYS 3205 - Business Intelligence 1**
- **BSYS 3355 - Management Information Systems**
- **BUSA 4850 - Consulting Skills & Problem Solving**
- **FMGT 3221 - Management Accounting Administration**
- **OPMT 3301 - Quantitative Methods for Business**

#### **Level 4 (Advanced Data Management)**
- **BABI 4000 - Applied Data Management for Analytics**
- **BABI 4005 - Data Quality Management with Python**
- **BLAW 3600 - Computers and the Law**
- **BSYS 4000 - Cloud Business Development 2**
- **BSYS 4905 - Directed Studies (Capstone Project)**
- **BUSA 4800 - Management Policy**
- **FMGT 4530 - Enterprise Finance**
- **OPMT 4170 - Applied Project Management**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **First-year completion required (common BITMAN entry)**.  
- **Laptop required (Windows 10 or newer, BYOD policy)**.  
- **English 12 & Math 12 (67% min.) or equivalent**.  
- **International Students:** **Study permit required**.

### **Career Paths**
- **Business Intelligence Analyst**  
- **Data Governance Analyst**  
- **Data Quality Manager**  
- **Data Architect & Database Administrator**  
- **Cloud Data Integration Specialist**

## **Business of Sawmilling, Associate Certificate, Distance & Online Learning (5125ACERT) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Part-time, Online Certificate in Industrial Wood Processing**.
- **Delivery:** **Fully Online (Self-Paced, Cohort-Based)**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Train professionals in **lumber industry business management**.
  - Develop skills in **finance, marketing, process optimization, and sustainability**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Lumber Economics & Market Analysis**  
- **Finance & Cost Control for Sawmill Managers**  
- **Process Optimization & Reliability**  
- **Sawmill Residual Management & By-Product Utilization**  
- **Lumber Sales & Marketing Strategies**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **May & October**.  
- **Completion timeline:** **12 months (Cohort Model)**.  
- **Schedule:** **Self-paced, one course at a time**.  

### **Core Courses**
- **ECON 1610 - Lumber Industry Economics**
- **FMGT 1660 - Finance Fundamentals for Sawmill Managers**
- **WOOD 2610 - Process Reliability & Optimization**
- **WOOD 2620 - Fundamentals of Sawmill Residuals**
- **MKTG 2760 - Lumber Sales & Marketing**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Applicants must be employed in the lumber industry & sponsored by an employer**.  
- **Recommended:** **English 10 & Math 10** (or self-assessment via WOOD 0002).  
- **International Students:** **Not eligible**.

### **Career Paths**
- **Sawmill Operations Supervisor**  
- **Lumber Industry Financial Analyst**  
- **Sawmill Sales & Marketing Manager**  
- **Wood Processing Business Consultant**

## **Canadian Engineering Professional Practice, Microcredential, Part-time (0852CM) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Engineering Law, Ethics & Licensing Microcredential**.
- **Delivery:** **Fully Online (Part-time)**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Prepare internationally trained engineers for **Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) licensure in Canada**.
  - Covers **engineering law, ethics, technical communication, and professional accountability**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Regulatory & Legal Framework for Engineers**  
- **Engineering Code of Ethics & Professional Responsibilities**  
- **Safety Awareness & Risk Management**  
- **Technical Writing & Oral Communication**  
- **National Professional Practice Examination (NPPE) Preparation**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**.  
- **Completion timeline:** **1 year (flexible, up to 7 years max.)**.  
- **Schedule:** **Evening Classes (18:30 – 21:30)**.  

### **Core Courses**
- **ENGR 4000 - Canadian Engineering Practical Law**
- **ENGR 4010 - Engineering Ethics & Professional Practice**
- **ENGR 4020 - Technical Communication for Engineers**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Diploma or degree in Engineering, Applied Science, or Engineering Technology required**.  
- **English 12 (50%) or equivalent**.  
- **International Students:** **Eligible if studying from outside Canada (not for study permit holders).**  

### **Career Paths**
- **Registered Professional Engineer (P.Eng.)**  
- **Engineering Compliance Specialist**  
- **Technical Consultant & Policy Advisor**  
- **Project Engineer (Legal & Regulatory Focus)**
