
## **Computer Systems, Diploma, Part-time (5500PDIPLT) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Part-time Diploma in Software Development & IT Systems**.  
- **Delivery:** **Blended (Evening & Weekend Classes)**.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Builds on **Computer Systems Certificate (CSC)**.  
  - Provides specialization in **Web Development, DevOps, Cloud Computing, and Cybersecurity**.  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Software Development (Java, C++, Python, C#)**
- **Cybersecurity & IT Infrastructure**
- **Cloud Computing (AWS, Azure)**
- **Database & Business Intelligence**
- **Network Administration & DevOps**

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**.  
- **Completion timeline:** **2-4 years (Flexible Learning)**.  

### **Core Courses**
- **COMP 3603 - Application Development (C++)**
- **COMP 3704 - Applied IT Security Fundamentals**
- **COMP 3725 - Data Communications for IT**
- **COMP 3761 - Algorithm Analysis & Design**
- **COMP 4737 - Operating Systems**
- **COMP 4603 - Advanced C++ Development**
- **LIBS 7102 - Ethics for Computing Professionals**

### **Electives (Specialization)**
- **Cloud Computing (AWS, Azure)**
- **Web Development (PHP, React, Node.js)**
- **Database & Business Intelligence**
- **Cybersecurity & Network Security**
- **Applied DevOps & Kubernetes**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Completion of Computer Systems Certificate (CSC) required**.  
- **International Students:** **Eligible with a valid study permit**.  

### **Career Paths**
- **Software Engineer**
- **Cloud Architect**
- **Cybersecurity Analyst**
- **DevOps Engineer**
- **Database Administrator**
