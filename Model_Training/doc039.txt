## **Cybersecurity Operations, Microcredential, Part-time (0832CM) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Cybersecurity Operations Microcredential**.  
- **Delivery:** **Fully Online (Part-time)**.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Introduces students to **security operations and incident response**.  
  - Prepares for roles in a **Security Operations Centre (SOC) as a Security Analyst or Incident Responder**.  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Network Attack Detection & Analysis**  
- **Cybersecurity Threats & Risk Mitigation**  
- **Incident Response Planning & Management**  
- **Cybersecurity Data Analytics & Forensics**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**.  
- **Completion timeline:** **Flexible, self-paced**.  

### **Core Courses**
- **XINC 2110 - Fundamentals of System and Network Attack Surfaces (2.0 credits)**
- **XINC 2120 - Fundamentals of Cybersecurity Threats and Attacks (1.0 credit)**
- **XINC 2130 - Fundamentals of Cybersecurity Network Defence (1.0 credit)**
- **XINC 2140 - Fundamentals of Cybersecurity Data Analysis (2.0 credits)**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Recommended:** Prior IT education or industry certification.  
- **International Students:** Eligible **only if studying from outside Canada (not for study permit holders)**.  

### **Career Paths**
- **Security Analyst (SOC Analyst)**
- **Cyber Incident Responder**
- **Network Security Engineer**
- **Cyber Threat Intelligence Specialist**
