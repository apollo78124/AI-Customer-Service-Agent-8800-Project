
## **Digital Design and Development, Diploma, Full-time (6515DIPMA) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Full-time Diploma in Digital Media & Web Development**  
- **Delivery:** **Blended (On-campus & Online)**  
- **Objective:**  
  - Trains students in **UI/UX, front-end web development, digital marketing, and multimedia content creation**  
  - Covers **designing, developing, and marketing digital applications across various platforms**  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **UI/UX Design & Prototyping**  
- **Frontend & Full-Stack Web Development**  
- **Motion Graphics & Video Production**  
- **Digital Branding & Marketing Strategies**  
- **Game & Interactive Content Design**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **September**  
- **Duration:** **2 years (4 levels, 110.5 total credits, including an 8-week practicum)**  

### **Core Courses**
#### **Year 1**
- **Frontend Web Technologies & JavaScript**  
- **Vector Graphics & Photoshop**  
- **Agile Project Management & Marketing**  

#### **Year 2**
- **Advanced Frontend Web Development**  
- **Motion Graphics & Dynamic Content Design**  
- **Full-Stack Web Development & IT Law**  
- **Final Capstone Project & Practicum (8 weeks)**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **English 12 (67%) or equivalent**  
- **Recommended:** Basic proficiency in Mac/Windows & digital design tools  
- **International Students:** **Eligible with a study permit (Co-op Work Permit required for practicum)**  

### **Career Paths**
- **UI/UX Designer**  
- **Frontend Web Developer**  
- **Digital Media Producer**  
- **Motion Graphics Designer**  
- **Web & Mobile Content Specialist**
