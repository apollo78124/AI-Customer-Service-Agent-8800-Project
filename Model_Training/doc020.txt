
## **Computer Systems Technology, Diploma, Full-time (5500DIPMA) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Full-time Diploma in Software Development & Computer Systems**.  
- **Delivery:** **In-person (Burnaby & Downtown Campus)**.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Trains students in **software engineering, system administration, and networking**.  
  - Offers **specializations in Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Web & Mobile Development, and Cybersecurity**.  
  - Provides **co-op education and industry projects**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Software Development & Programming (Java, C, C++, Python)**
- **Data Structures & Algorithms**
- **Cloud Computing & DevOps**
- **Database & Full-Stack Web Development**
- **Networking & Cybersecurity**
- **Machine Learning & AI Applications**

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **September & January**.  
- **Duration:** **2 years (4 levels, 120 credits total)**.  
- **Co-op & Industry Projects:** **Available for work experience**.  

### **Specializations (Second Year)**
- **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning**
- **Cloud Computing & DevOps**
- **Web & Mobile Development**
- **Cybersecurity & Network Engineering**
- **Predictive Analytics**
- **Database & Full-Stack Development**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **English 12 (67%) & Pre-Calculus 12 (67%) or equivalent**.  
- **Preferred:** **Computer Science 11 or 12**.  
- **International Students:** **Eligible with a study permit**.  

### **Career Paths**
- **Software Engineer**
- **Data Scientist & AI Engineer**
- **Network Security Engineer**
- **Cloud & DevOps Engineer**
- **Database Administrator**
- **Game Developer & UI/UX Specialist**
