
## **Digital Design and Development, Diploma, Full-time (6515DIPMA) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Full-time Diploma in Digital Design & Development**.  
- **Delivery:** **Blended (On-campus & Online)**.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Train students in **UI/UX, front-end web development, digital marketing, and multimedia design**.  
  - Prepare for careers in **web development, game design, digital media, and interactive content creation**.  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Frontend & Full-Stack Web Development**
- **UI/UX & Motion Graphics**
- **Digital Branding & Marketing**
- **Video & Audio Production for Digital Media**  
- **Mobile & Interactive Content Development**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **September**.  
- **Duration:** **2 years (4 levels, including an 8-week practicum).**  
- **Schedule:** **Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM**.  

### **Core Courses**
#### **Year 1**
- **Fundamentals of Web & Mobile Development**
- **Vector Graphics & Digital Illustration**
- **Project Management & Agile Development**
- **Marketing & Branding for Digital Media**

#### **Year 2**
- **Advanced Frontend Web Development**
- **Motion Graphics & Animation**
- **Dynamic Content & Interactive Design**
- **Capstone Project & Practicum (8 weeks)**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **English 12 (67%) or equivalent**.  
- **Basic proficiency in Mac/Windows & design tools recommended**.  
- **International Students:** **Eligible with a study permit (Co-op Work Permit required for practicum).**  

### **Career Paths**
- **UI/UX Designer**
- **Frontend Web Developer**
- **Digital Media Producer**
- **Motion Graphics Designer**
- **Game UI Designer**
