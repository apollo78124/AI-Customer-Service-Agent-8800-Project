
## **Building Energy Modelling and Simulation, Microcredential, Part-time (0829CM) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Energy Modelling & Simulation Microcredential**.
- **Delivery:** **Online (Part-time)**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Train students to become **Building Energy Modellers**.
  - Teach **whole-building energy simulations for design & retrofit decisions**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Energy Modelling Fundamentals**  
- **Building Performance Simulation**  
- **High-Performance Building Design**  
- **HVAC & Mechanical System Modelling**  
- **Energy Efficiency & Sustainable Design Practices**  

### **Core Courses**
- **XBEM 3050 - Building Energy Modelling & Simulation Fundamentals**
- **XBEM 3052 - Advanced Building Performance Simulation**
- **XBEM 3054 - High-Performance Building Design Project**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Intended for working professionals with prior education or experience in building energy systems**.  
- **International Students:** **Eligible for online completion (not for study permit holders).**
