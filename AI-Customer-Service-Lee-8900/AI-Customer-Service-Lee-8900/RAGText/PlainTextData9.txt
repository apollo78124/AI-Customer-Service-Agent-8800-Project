

## **Cybersecurity Operations, Microcredential, Part-time (0832CM) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Cybersecurity Operations Microcredential**  
- **Delivery:** **Fully Online (Part-time, Self-Paced)**  
- **Objective:**  
  - Provides foundational training in **Security Operations Center (SOC) tasks**  
  - Prepares students for roles such as **Security Analyst or Incident Responder**  
  - Develops skills in **network attack detection, cybersecurity analysis, and incident response planning**  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Network Attack Surfaces & Risk Analysis**  
- **Threat Detection & Cybersecurity Defense**  
- **Incident Response & Forensic Data Analysis**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**  
- **Completion timeline:** **Self-paced, part-time study**  
- **Total Credits:** **6.0**  

### **Core Courses**
- **XINC 2110 - Fundamentals of System and Network Attack Surfaces (2.0 credits)**  
- **XINC 2120 - Fundamentals of Cybersecurity Threats and Attacks (1.0 credit)**  
- **XINC 2130 - Fundamentals of Cybersecurity Network Defence (1.0 credit)**  
- **XINC 2140 - Fundamentals of Cybersecurity Data Analysis (2.0 credits)**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Recommended:** Prior IT education, diploma, degree, or cybersecurity certification  
- **International Students:** **Eligible if studying from outside Canada (not for study permit holders)**  

### **Career Paths**
- **Security Analyst (SOC Analyst)**  
- **Cyber Incident Responder**  
- **Network Security Engineer**  
- **Threat Intelligence Analyst**

## **Digital Design and Development, Diploma, Full-time (6515DIPMA) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Full-time Diploma in Digital Media & Web Development**  
- **Delivery:** **Blended (On-campus & Online)**  
- **Objective:**  
  - Trains students in **UI/UX, front-end web development, digital marketing, and multimedia content creation**  
  - Covers **designing, developing, and marketing digital applications across various platforms**  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **UI/UX Design & Prototyping**  
- **Frontend & Full-Stack Web Development**  
- **Motion Graphics & Video Production**  
- **Digital Branding & Marketing Strategies**  
- **Game & Interactive Content Design**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **September**  
- **Duration:** **2 years (4 levels, 110.5 total credits, including an 8-week practicum)**  

### **Core Courses**
#### **Year 1**
- **Frontend Web Technologies & JavaScript**  
- **Vector Graphics & Photoshop**  
- **Agile Project Management & Marketing**  

#### **Year 2**
- **Advanced Frontend Web Development**  
- **Motion Graphics & Dynamic Content Design**  
- **Full-Stack Web Development & IT Law**  
- **Final Capstone Project & Practicum (8 weeks)**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **English 12 (67%) or equivalent**  
- **Recommended:** Basic proficiency in Mac/Windows & digital design tools  
- **International Students:** **Eligible with a study permit (Co-op Work Permit required for practicum)**  

### **Career Paths**
- **UI/UX Designer**  
- **Frontend Web Developer**  
- **Digital Media Producer**  
- **Motion Graphics Designer**  
- **Web & Mobile Content Specialist**

## **Essentials of Data Networks, Microcredential, Part-time (0811CM) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Microcredential in Networking Fundamentals**  
- **Delivery:** **Fully Online (Part-time, Self-Paced)**  
- **Objective:**  
  - Introduces **basic networking concepts**  
  - Prepares students for **entry-level networking roles**  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Network Fundamentals & Terminology**  
- **IP Addressing & Network Protocols**  
- **Network Connectivity & Security Basics**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**  
- **Completion timeline:** **Self-paced, part-time**  
- **Total Credits:** **6.0**  

### **Core Courses**
- **XINC 1101 - Network Terminology and Protocols (2.0 credits)**  
- **XINC 1102 - Network Addressing and Services (2.0 credits)**  
- **XINC 1103 - Network Connectivity (2.0 credits)**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Minimum Requirement:** Grade 10 completion  
- **Recommended:** Basic computer literacy & web browsing skills  
- **International Students:** **Eligible if studying from outside Canada (not for study permit holders)**  

### **Career Paths**
- **Network Administrator (Entry-Level)**  
- **Data Center Technician**  
- **IT Support Specialist**
