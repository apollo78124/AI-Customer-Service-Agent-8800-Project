
## **Applied Network Administration and Design, Associate Certificate, Part-time (5515ACERT) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Network Administration & Security Specialization**.
- **Delivery:** **In-person (Burnaby & Downtown Campus)**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Train students in **designing, managing, and securing enterprise networks**.
  - Cover **Windows/Linux server administration, virtualization, and cloud security**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Windows & Linux Server Administration**
- **Network Security & Cybersecurity Foundations**
- **Cloud & Virtualization Technologies**
- **Scripting for Network Automation**
- **Disaster Recovery & IT Governance**

### **Program Structure**
- **Intakes:** **January, April, September**.
- **Completion timeline:** **2-4 years**.
- **Schedule:** **Evening & weekend courses**.

### **Core Courses**
- **COMP 1002 - Applied Computer Concepts (3.0 credits)**
- **COMP 2629 - Enterprise Networking Fundamentals (4.0 credits)**
- **COMP 2766 - Introduction to Linux (4.0 credits)**
- **COMP 3677 - Windows Server Administration 1 (1.5 credits)**
- **COMP 3681 - Windows Server Administration 2 (1.5 credits)**
- **COMP 3704 - IT Security Fundamentals (3.0 credits)**
- **COMP 4912 - Applied Virtual Networks (3.0 credits)**

### **Elective Courses (Choose 3.0 Credits)**
- **COMP 2767 - Linux Administration**
- **COMP 3771 - PowerShell Scripting**
- **COMP 4704 - Applied Network Security**

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Prerequisites:**  
  - **Prior IT experience or post-secondary education in computing**.  
  - **Mathematics & English proficiency (Grade 12 or equivalent)**.  
- **International Students:**  
  - **Not available for study permit holders**.
