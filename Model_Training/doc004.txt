
## **Applied Computer Applications (ACA), Associate Certificate (5525ACERT) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Office & productivity software training program**.
- **Delivery:** **Blended (Online + In-Person)**.
- **Status:** **Under review, not accepting applications**.
- **Objective:**  
  - Provide expert-level training in **Windows 11, Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), and basic IT concepts**.
  - Includes **optional courses in IT project management, reporting tools (Crystal Reports), and business communication**.

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Operating System & File Management (Windows 11)**  
- **Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access)**  
- **Web Development Basics**  
- **Business & Technical Communication**  
- **IT Project Management & Reporting Tools**

### **Core Courses (13.0 Credits)**
- **COMP 1002 - Applied Computer Concepts (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 1850 - Introduction to Web Development (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 2364 - Microsoft Excel (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 2369 - Microsoft Word (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMP 2384 - Microsoft PowerPoint (1.0 credit)**  

### **Elective Courses (Choose 4.5 credits)**
- **COMP 1288 - IT Project Management Fundamentals (1.5 credits)**  
- **COMP 1370 - Microsoft Access Fundamentals (1.5 credits)**  
- **COMP 2252 - SAP Crystal Reports (3.0 credits)**  
- **COMM 1103 or COMM 1106 - Business & Technical Communication (3.0 credits)**  
- **XCOM 3840 - Technical Presentations (1.5 credits)**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **Prerequisite:** **English 12 (or equivalent knowledge in reading, writing, and speaking)**.  
- **International students:** **Not eligible**.  

### **Costs & Timeline**
- **Estimated cost:** **$4,000 (varies by electives chosen)**.  
- **Completion timeline:** **3+ part-time terms**.  
- **Location:** **Burnaby Campus, Downtown Campus, Online**.
