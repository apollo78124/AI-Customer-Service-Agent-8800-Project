
## **Chemical and Environmental Technology (Analytical Science Option), Diploma, Full-time (537ADIPLT) - BCIT**
### **Program Overview**
- **Type:** **Full-time Diploma in Chemical & Environmental Analysis**.  
- **Delivery:** **In-person (Burnaby Campus)**.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Provides a strong foundation in **instrumental analysis, organic chemistry, and environmental regulations**.  
  - Prepares students for careers in **environmental analysis, industrial research, and laboratory technology**.  

### **Key Learning Areas**
- **Instrumental Laboratory Analysis**  
- **Organic Chemistry & Chemical Regulations**  
- **Environmental Sampling & Pollution Control**  
- **Fire Assaying & Classical Chemical Analysis**  
- **Physical Chemistry & Data Interpretation**  

### **Program Structure**
- **Intake:** **September**.  
- **Duration:** **2 years (4 levels, including practicum & applied research project)**.  
- **Schedule:** **Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM**.  
- **Work Placement:** **Mandatory industry practicum (Co-op Work Permit required for international students).**  

### **Core Courses**
#### **Year 1 (Common Core with Process Engineering)**
- **CENV 1101 - Safety & Technology Workshop**  
- **CENV 1119 - Environmental Science**  
- **CHEM 1121 - General Chemistry**  
- **PHYS 1181 - Physics for Chemical & Environmental Tech**  
- **MATH 1412 - Technical Mathematics**  

#### **Year 2 (Analytical Science Specialization)**
- **CENV 3312 - Waste Management**  
- **CENV 3313 - Environmental Sampling & Analysis**  
- **CENV 3318 - Classical Analysis & Fire Assaying**  
- **CHEM 3409 - Organic Chemistry 2**  
- **CENV 4418 - Instrumental Inorganic Analysis**  
- **CENV 4427 - Instrumental Organic Analysis**  
- **CENV 4401 - Industry Practicum**  

### **Admissions & Requirements**
- **English 12 (67%) & Math 12 (73%) or equivalent**.  
- **Chemistry 11 (73%)**.  
- **International Students:** **Study permit required + Co-op Work Permit for practicum**.  

### **Career Paths**
- **Environmental & Laboratory Analyst**  
- **Chemical Technician in Industrial R&D**  
- **Quality Control & Process Monitoring**  
- **Regulatory & Compliance Officer**
